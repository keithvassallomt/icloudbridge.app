iCloudBridge

A small window into Apple's walled garden. Keep your Notes, Reminders, Photos and Passwords in sync with the tools you actually use.

Download GitHub

Sync Notes

Sync Apple Notes, with support for folders, attachments, images, text formatting and even partial checklists!

Sync Reminders

Sync Apple Reminders, with support for alarms, recurring reminders, time zones and multiple reminder lists.

Sync Passwords

Sync your Apple Passwords with Bitwarden, Vaultwarden or Nextcloud Passwords.

Sync Photos

Sync photos from non-Apple devices to your Apple Photos library.

Schedules & Logs

Schedules: Create recurring schedules to automatically sync notes, reminders and photos.

Logs: Keep a close eye on everything iCloudBridge is doing, in real time.
